Welcome to B'S Barber

Welcome to B'S Barber, where tradition meets modern style. Founded in 2024, we are committed to providing exceptional grooming services tailored to the unique needs of each client. Our team of skilled barbers is dedicated to delivering precision cuts, classic shaves, and contemporary styles in a relaxed, welcoming environment.

At B'S Barber, we believe that a great haircut can change your outlook and boost your confidence. Shaving

Basic Shave

Our basic shave includes a straightforward, clean shave using high-quality shaving cream and disposable razors. This option is perfect for a quick and efficient grooming session.

RM 15
Premium Shave

The premium shave offers a more luxurious experience. It includes a hot towel treatment, a professional straight razor shave, and the use of premium shaving products. This option provides a closer, smoother shave with added relaxation and care for your skin.

RM 30
Ultimate Shave Experience

The ultimate shave experience is our top-tier service. It includes all the benefits of the premium shave, plus an exfoliating pre-shave treatment, a moisturizing post-shave balm, and a facial massage. This option is ideal for those looking to indulge in a complete grooming and relaxation experience, leaving your skin feeling rejuvenated and refreshed.
